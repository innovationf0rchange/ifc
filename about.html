Our Story

About Innovation For Change

A cultural movement born in the heart of Madhubani, Bihar — giving every unheard voice a stage, a spotlight, and an audience.

How It Began

A Revolution in the Heart of Bihar

Innovation For Change was born from a simple but powerful idea — to give a stage to the unheard voices of Bihar. It started as a small gathering in Madhubani and soon transformed into a vibrant platform for youth, artists, and creators to express themselves freely.

By organizing dozens of successful open mic events, we've created not just performances but a cultural movement — reintroducing the spirit of art in Bihar and nurturing local talent that had long deserved a spotlight.

Innovation For Change isn't just a stage — it's a spotlight. We provide professional setups, photography, wide social media reach, and an encouraging crowd that believes in your voice.

"A single voice can inspire many, a connected community can transform society."

— Abhinash Jha, Director

From Maithili poetry and rap to stand-up comedy and mimicry, our platform celebrates every form of creative expression. We believe art is not a luxury — it is a right.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To create an open, inclusive, and professional platform for artists, poets, singers, storytellers, and comedians in Bihar — especially those from smaller towns who have never had a stage.

🌟

Our Vision

A Bihar where every young artist has the confidence to perform, the community to support them, and the platform to grow — becoming the largest open mic movement in Mithilanchal and across India.
